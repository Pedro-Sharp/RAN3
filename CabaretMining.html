Mining Policies & Rules

  • Moons Belts will be available from Monday 18h EVE Time till Wednesday 19 EVE Time.
  • The use of Waste Modules is forbidden on R16 and R4 asteroids. Such modules are however tolerated on R8 asteroids.
  • The Corporation will charge a 10% tax on raw mined ore, which is to be contracted to Sharp Tsero until Sunday each week.
  • If you are flying a Rorqual, you should be aware of defensive procedures in case of hostile attacks.
  • If you are flying a Rorqual, you should be aware that the alliance will NOT provide a defensive fleet in case of hostile attacks during Stratecic Ops, especially during War Time.
